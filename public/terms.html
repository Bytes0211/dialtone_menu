03 Orders and reservations

An order is considered placed only after the caller confirms the full readback from the voice agent. Payment is collected by the restaurant via a secure Stripe Checkout link sent by SMS; DialTone does not accept card information over the phone. Reservations are confirmed when the agent speaks the confirmation message and an SMS confirmation is sent. The restaurant has the right to refuse or cancel any order or reservation at its discretion, including for reasons of capacity, payment failure, or clearly erroneous input.

04 SMS messaging

By placing an order or booking a reservation through DialTone, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages at the phone number you called from — specifically: a payment link for card orders, reservation confirmations, and reservation reminders. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies and is typically limited to one or two messages per order or booking.

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message from DialTone. Reply HELP for assistance. Opting out will prevent DialTone from sending a payment link, which means card orders placed by phone cannot be completed — you'll need to pay at the restaurant in person instead. DialTone does not use SMS for marketing and does not share phone numbers with third parties for their marketing.

05 Payments and refunds

Refunds, price adjustments, and order disputes are the responsibility of the restaurant that took the order — contact the restaurant directly if you need to resolve an order issue.

For cash orders placed in person after being initiated over the phone, payment terms are handled between you and the restaurant at pickup.
